CAS HR Portal

CAS HR Portal is a platform designed to simplify request approvals, performance reviews, employee requests (leave, discounts, loans, etc.), and employee records by bringing them into one cohesive dashboard.

Role
Product Designer
Team
2 PMs, 6 Developers
Duration
12 Weeks (3 Months)
Overview

CAS HR Portal is a platform built to integrate legacy systems, payrolls, request approvals, performance review management, and employee records in one place. It helps HR managers handle employee requests efficiently, covering leave applications, expense approvals, appraisals, announcements, and training management all under one unified system.

The Problem

Before CAS HR Portal, staff members and employees could not easily make requests, verify their statuses, or get fast approvals. Managers also struggled to monitor and track their team members' requests. The majority of these requests were processed manually using paper forms, leading to data security risks, slow processing turnarounds, and missing documentation.

The Solution

I designed an intuitive, responsive web and mobile application that handles request management for employees, managers, and HR teams. The final platform standardizes approval workflows to increase corporate transparency and automatically channels financial requests to the appropriate departments for validation.

The Goal

Build a digital workspace that aims to:

  • Track and manage employee requests seamlessly
  • Standardize the multi-tier request approval flow
  • Keep records of employee performance and appraisals
  • Provide a comprehensive set of management tools for the HR team
Users
Employees
Want a simple, transparent way to file requests (leave, discounts, expense reimbursement) and track their progress in real time.
Line Managers
Need a clear dashboard to review pending requests, compare team availability, and approve or reject submissions quickly.
HR Teams
Require an automated portal to oversee compliance, manage employee profiles, track approvals, and manage performance logs.

Conclusion & Outcomes

The CAS HR Portal is a comprehensive platform that simplifies request approvals, performance management, and employee record management. By replacing paper-based processes with standard digital workflows, the app improves communication, reduces processing time, and increases transparency for employees and HR teams. Overall, this project helped me design complex dashboards and payroll systems, balancing technical requirements with a seamless user experience.
