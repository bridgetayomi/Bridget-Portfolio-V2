About Me

Product designer crafting experiences people actually enjoy.

I'm a product designer who cares deeply about how things look and how they work. I create clean, intuitive digital experiences with just the right balance of logic and personality.

Whether I'm wireframing dashboards, refining mobile flows, or explaining why tooltips aren't meant to hold entire paragraphs 😅. I'm always thinking about the people behind the screen.

I'm currently based in Lagos, Nigeria, and I've worked on products in fintech, healthcare, e-commerce and security, collaborating closely with developers, PMs, and everyday users to bring ideas to life. I believe good design is less about making things pretty and more about making them work beautifully.

I'm big on communication, fast feedback, and making sure no user is left confused. Outside of work, you'll probably find me watching movies, making TikTok videos, gisting and catching up with friends or learning something new.
