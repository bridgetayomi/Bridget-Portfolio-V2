Helpnest — Business Hours settings

A minimal brief to "improve the Business Hours settings page" turned into a fully interactive mockup: sidebar navigation, tabbed settings, per-day schedule toggles with AM/PM dropdowns, dynamic status badges, and a live weekly coverage bar.

Overview

The brief

Helpnest, a customer support platform, needed its Business Hours settings page improved. The original brief was minimal — [describe what was actually specified] — which left most of the interaction design and edge cases up to me.

Problem

Where the original fell short

  • No clear at-a-glance read of which days were covered and which weren't.
  • Setting hours per day required repetitive, error-prone input with no live feedback.
  • [Add another specific gap you identified.]
Process

What I did

  • Added sidebar navigation and tabbed settings so Business Hours sits inside a coherent settings structure, not as an isolated page.
  • Built per-day schedule toggles with AM/PM dropdowns for precise, low-friction input.
  • Designed dynamic status badges (Open / Closed / Custom) that update as hours change.
  • Added a live weekly coverage bar so support managers can see total coverage at a glance.
