GSCS Integrated

A full website redesign for GSCS Integrated — a clearing & forwarding, warehousing, and pest control company — after the client felt their old site didn't explain what they actually offered.

Role
Product Designer
Duration
4 Weeks
Year
2024
Overview

GSCS Integrated provides clearing and forwarding, warehousing, and pest control services for businesses across Nigeria and beyond. Despite offering reliable logistics and compliance-driven solutions, their previous website was cluttered, text-heavy, and lacked a clear user journey. Potential clients struggled to quickly understand their offerings, and the overall digital experience didn't reflect the company's professionalism.

The Problem
  • Overloaded Content: the website had large blocks of text with little hierarchy, making it overwhelming to read.
  • Poor User Experience: users had no clear navigation path to explore services like clearing, warehousing, and pest control.
  • Lack of Visual Appeal: the absence of engaging visuals and structured layouts made the brand feel less credible.
  • Weak Conversion Flow: calls to action were either buried in text or not compelling enough to encourage inquiries or requests for quotes.

The Goal

The goal was to redesign GSCS Integrated's website into a modern, user-friendly platform that clearly communicates their services, builds trust with prospects, and drives conversions through clear calls to action.

The Solution

The redesign delivered a clean, structured, and conversion-focused platform by reorganizing services into clear sections, improving visual hierarchy, and unifying the design language. Strong calls-to-action were added to drive inquiries, while certifications, endorsements, and compliance details were highlighted to build trust and credibility.
