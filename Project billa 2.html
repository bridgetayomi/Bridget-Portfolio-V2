My Billa

An online marketplace for unique digital products — from ebooks and webinars to utility payments and exam pins. Built to simplify access for both buyers and sellers.

Role
Product Designer
Team
2 PMs, 2 Developers
Duration
8 Weeks
Overview

An online marketplace for digital products — from ebooks and webinars to utility services like airtime, data, and bill payments. I worked on the website, mobile app, and dashboard experiences, helping the team streamline complex user flows into something simple and intuitive.

The Goal

Build a digital platform that lets users purchase multiple services — like airtime, data, and utility payments — all at once. This all-in-one experience, powered by the Billa Bucket, sets Billa apart from most platforms where users have to buy each service separately. The goal was to reduce friction, save time, and create a smooth, bundled checkout flow that feels intuitive and fast.

The Challenge

Users were used to making one-off purchases on other platforms. There was no easy way to buy everything they needed in one session, which led to repetitive, frustrating flows.

Process

Research

I dug deep to understand what users really need, gathering insights that helped shape Billa into the right tool for them. Here's what I found:

Person 1"I need a secure, all-in-one platform to pay for data, airtime, and power in one go."
Person 2"A simple, fast way to manage all my payments in one place would save me time."
Person 3"A simple platform with reminders and easy payment options would help me stay organized."
Person 4"I'd love to have quick access to digital content while managing my bills seamlessly."
