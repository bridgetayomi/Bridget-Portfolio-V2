My Billa

An online marketplace for unique digital products — from ebooks and webinars to utility payments and exam pins. Built to simplify access for both buyers and sellers.

Overview

The brief

Billa needed a marketplace that could hold two very different kinds of transactions — digital products like ebooks and webinars, and everyday utility payments like exam pins and bill top-ups — without making either feel like an afterthought.

[Add a sentence on why you took this on and what "done" looked like for the project.]

Problem

Where the challenge was

  • Buyers needed to trust and browse a marketplace, while also completing quick, transactional tasks like paying a bill — two different mental modes on one platform.
  • Sellers needed a clear, low-friction way to list and manage digital products without needing technical or web development knowledge.
  • [Add another specific gap you identified.]
Process

What I did

  • Designed a marketplace-first landing experience that leads with trust signals (Speed, Convenience, Optimized) before asking buyers to commit to an action.
  • Structured navigation around the two core use cases — Marketplace and Bills Payments — so neither one is buried under the other.
  • Prototyped the flow across desktop and mobile so selling and paying bills both feel native to their screen size, not just scaled down.
