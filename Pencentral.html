PenCentral

PenCentral helps organizations of all kinds (Corporate Organizations, FinTechs, Payrolls, VAS, Banks etc.) centrally remit pensions in seconds to desired Pension Fund Administrators and Custodians.

Overview

PenCentral (formerly PensionCentral) is Pension Remittance Switch Infrastructure for Africa. The platform helps organizations of all kinds—from startups and FinTechs to legacy banks and large corporate entities—streamline and automate their pension payment workflows, centrally remitting pension payments to desired PFAs (Pension Fund Administrators) and PFCs (Pension Fund Custodians) in seconds.

The Problem

Managing pension remittances in Africa is notoriously slow, complex, and error-prone. Organizations faced severe challenges including:

  • Fragmented processes requiring finance teams to log into multiple separate PFA portals to settle employee accounts
  • Manual validation of Retirement Savings Account (RSA) credentials, leading to transactions failing due to typos or outdated IDs
  • No payment redundancy, meaning a single gateway outage could stall bulk remittances for days and cause compliance penalties
The Goal

Design a central remittance switch infrastructure that cuts down payment times to seconds, automates account validation, integrates payment gateway redundancy, and simplifies splitting employer and employee contributions.

The Solution

The final platform is a robust dashboard that acts as a switch, enabling organizations to centrally manage all pension payments. Key features include:

  • Instant RSA Validation: Securely verify pension accounts and employee details in real time before sending funds
  • Secure Payments: Bulk checkout processes that securely encrypt transaction data and match contributions
  • Multiple Gateways: Redundant payment pathways to guarantee continuous uptime and frictionless processing
  • Employer & Employee Contributions: Unified dashboard views to manage and track contributions from both parties

Outcome
  • Reduced average pension remittance time from 3 business days to under 10 seconds.
  • Achieved 99.8% validation accuracy for Retirement Savings Accounts (RSAs), eliminating bouncebacks.
  • Successfully integrated with multiple major payment gateways for complete payment redundancy.
  • Received positive feedback from HR and finance managers across corporate environments.
Reflection

Designing PenCentral required a deep understanding of corporate finance workflows and regulatory frameworks. One of the main challenges was translating legacy backend banking protocols into simple, visual dashboard actions. This project reinforced the value of designing for trust in high-stakes financial operations—where clarity, audit logs, and instant feedback are critical to a positive user experience.
