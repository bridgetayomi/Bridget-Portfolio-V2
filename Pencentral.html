Overview

PenCentral (formerly PensionCentral) is a pension remittance switch infrastructure for Africa. The platform helps organisations of all kinds, from startups and FinTechs to legacy banks and large corporate entities, streamline and automate their pension payment workflows, centrally remitting pension payments to desired PFAs (Pension Fund Administrators) and PFCs (Pension Fund Custodians) in seconds.

The Problem

Managing pension remittances in Nigeria is notoriously slow, complex, and error-prone. Organisations faced severe challenges including:

  • Fragmented processes requiring finance teams to log into multiple separate PFA portals to settle employee accounts
  • Manual validation of Retirement Savings Account (RSA) credentials, leading to transactions failing due to typos or outdated IDs
  • No payment redundancy, meaning a single gateway outage could stall bulk remittances for days and cause compliance penalties
The Goal

Design a central remittance switch infrastructure that cuts down payment times to seconds, automates account validation, integrates payment gateway redundancy, and simplifies splitting employer and employee contributions.

The Solution

The final platform is a robust dashboard that acts as a switch, enabling organisations to centrally manage all pension payments. Key features include:

  • Instant RSA Validation: Securely verify pension accounts and employee details in real time before sending funds
  • Secure Payments: Bulk checkout processes that securely encrypt transaction data and match contributions
  • Multiple Gateways: Redundant payment pathways to guarantee continuous uptime and frictionless processing
  • Employer & Employee Contributions: Unified dashboard views to manage and track contributions from both parties

Outcome
  • Reduced average pension remittance time from 3 business days to under 10 seconds.
  • Achieved 99.8% validation accuracy for Retirement Savings Accounts (RSAs), eliminating bouncebacks.
  • Successfully integrated with multiple major payment gateways for complete payment redundancy.
  • Received positive feedback from HR and finance managers across corporate environments.
Reflection

Designing PenCentral taught me a lot about working on products where accuracy and trust really matter. I had to understand how pension payments work, how different parts of the system connect, and how to turn complex processes into a dashboard that feels simple and easy to use.

One of the biggest things I learned was that when people are dealing with money, every detail matters. Clear information, helpful feedback, and knowing what is happening at every step can make a big difference in how confident users feel using a product.

Working on the AI assistant gave me a completely different challenge. I had to think about the different ways people might ask questions, how the assistant should respond, and what should happen when it doesn't have the right answer. It also made me think more carefully about how to make AI interactions feel simple and easy for users to understand.
